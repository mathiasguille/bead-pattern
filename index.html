      Frequently Asked Questions

      How long does it take to build a pattern?

      A 18√ó18 pattern (324 beads) typically takes 2-3 hours for beginners. Larger patterns (32√ó32, 40√ó40) can take 8-12+ hours for photorealistic results.

      What image types work best?

      High-contrast images, logos, silhouettes, and drawings work best. Photos with soft gradients may lose detail at smaller grid sizes (use 24√ó24 or larger).

      Can I use any color bead?

      Yes! Our tool includes Basic (11 colors), Pastel, and Bright palettes. You can adapt the pattern to beads you already have on hand.

      Is there a limit to image size?

      No limit! We process images of any size. The grid dimensions determine the final detail level, not the source image resolution.
